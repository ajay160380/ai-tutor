Refund Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We strongly believe in the quality of our AI tutoring platform. If you are not completely satisfied with your Pro or Premium subscription, we offer a "No Questions Asked" 7-day money-back guarantee for all first-time purchases.

2. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund within the 7-day window, simply send an email to support@eduai.in from your registered email address with the subject "Refund Request". Your request will be processed within 24-48 hours.

3. Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are credited back to the original payment method. Depending on your bank or UPI provider, it may take 5 to 7 business days for the amount to reflect in your account.

4. Exceptions

Renewals of subscriptions are not eligible for the 7-day money-back guarantee. You must cancel your subscription before the renewal date if you do not wish to be charged for the subsequent month.

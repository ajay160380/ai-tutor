Our Story 📖

EduAI started with a simple dream — that every Indian student should get a world-class education, no matter where they are.

🎯 Our Mission

Making the best quality education accessible to every student in India — whether in a tier 1 city or tier 3. Personalized tutoring through AI that truly understands what the student is struggling with.

👁️ Hamara Vision

2030 tak 1 crore Indian students ko free, quality AI-powered education dena. Coaching ki monopoly todna aur har ghar mein ek smart tutor pahunchana jo 24/7 available ho.

💡 Why We Built EduAI?

India has over 400 million students, but quality education is limited to a few cities. Coaching center fees are out of budget for an average family. EduAI fills this gap with AI technology — starting for free, with affordable premium features.

We have designed EduAI specifically for Indian students — English and regional language support, Indian examples (cricket, chai, biryani!), and exactly aligned with JEE/NEET patterns. This is not just a chatbot — it's your personal AI tutor.
